Our Story

Crafted for those who seek the extraordinary

Born from a passion for pure, potent beauty, LUMINA was founded on the belief that luxury should be accessible — and that every ritual deserves the finest ingredients the world has to offer.

2014

The Beginning

LUMINA started in a small atelier in Mumbai, with two founders and a vision to create skincare that merged ancient wisdom with cutting-edge science.

2017

First Breakthrough

Our signature serum won the Beauty Innovation Award, putting LUMINA on the global map and introducing us to 50,000+ new customers across Asia.

2020

Going Digital

We launched our flagship online store, making premium beauty accessible to customers across India and 15 countries worldwide.

2024

A New Chapter

With over 2 million happy customers and a portfolio of 200+ products, LUMINA continues to push the boundaries of beauty excellence.
